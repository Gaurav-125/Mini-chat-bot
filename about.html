About KIIT University

Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT), located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, is one of India's leading private universities. Established in 1992 by Dr. Achyuta Samanta, KIIT has grown into a globally recognized institution offering world-class education and research opportunities.

The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in fields such as engineering, management, law, medicine, biotechnology, computer science, and humanities.

KIIT is known for its modern infrastructure, international collaborations, advanced research facilities, and vibrant campus life. Students from more than 60 countries study at KIIT, making it a truly global educational hub.
