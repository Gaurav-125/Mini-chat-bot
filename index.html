About KIIT

Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT), located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, is a premier private university in India. Established in 1992 by Achyuta Samanta, KIIT offers a wide range of programs in engineering, management, law, medicine, and sciences. With modern infrastructure, international collaborations, and students from over 60 countries, KIIT has become a globally recognized center for education, research, and innovation.
